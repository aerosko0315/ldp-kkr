Make Your Countertop Durable & Stable

Skilled KKR staff will make your sheet-required countertop using the right type of fabrication technique prevent deformation. For instance, the bespoke-size countertop may need plywood underneath with appropriate thickness as the upholder to make sure it is stable.

Recognized as the professional kitchen Corian countertop manufacturer, KKR follows strict manufacturing requirements and uphold the production standards, which results in the long-lasting and durable bathroom and kitchen products that will make any home interior attractive.

Supporting all KKR countertops and vanity tops are lumber cores that conform with E0 standards. The lumber core makes our cut-to-size solid surfaces more stable than competing products that only follow E1 or E2 construction standards.

With the stability of our countertop and vanity tops as well as different lumber core options, Kingkonree can help you satisfy any construction requirements.

Size (Thickness)

Installing suitable solid surfaces in any interior space is made easy with the different size options of KKR custom countertop, including 20mm, 25 mm, 30mm, 40mm, 50mm.

More than 300 colors available for each KKR custom countertop. A group of colorists and a series of advanced CNC machines stand by to create and fabricate the pattern you want, be it intricate or not.

Surface finishes like matt and glossy cater to the design needs of any client and for any application. Shapes like round, square, L type and oval are available depending on your design.
